## 🖌 Overview  

Pro Tools Professional Suite is the complete professional audio production environment that has shaped how recorded music and post-production audio sounds for decades. It is the DAW that trained engineers expect to find in any serious studio environment.

Track counts in the hundreds, complex mix buses, and full plugin automation operate with the precision and headroom that broadcast and theatrical delivery specifications demand.

MIDI production tools handle everything from basic note entry to complex controller automation, virtual instrument hosting, and score output, making Pro Tools Professional Suite capable across both tracking and production workflows.

Bounce and delivery tools are designed around professional mastering and delivery requirements, with support for industry-standard formats, loudness standards, and metadata embedding that ensures your output is ready for any distribution channel.

Pro Tools Professional Suite is the audio production investment that working professionals make when they need their workflow to be compatible with every professional studio and delivery pipeline in the world.
